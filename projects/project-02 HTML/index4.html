10 BORING BRAIN TEASER QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

2. What occurs once in every minute, twice in every moment, yet never in a thousand years? The letter “M”.

3. What’s full of holes but still holds water? A sponge.

4. What word looks the same upside down and backward? SWIMS

5. What five letter word becomes shorter when you add two letters to it? The word short.

6. What can you keep but cannot share and once you share it, you can’t keep it anymore? A secret.

7. You leave home and go to your right. You reach a corner and turn left. You reach another corner and turn left again. You reach another corner and turn left again and go home. When you get there, there is a person with a mask there waiting for you. What’s happening? You are playing baseball.

8. The more it dries, the wetter it gets. What is it? A Towel.

9. There is only one time in your life when you’re twice as old as your child. When is that? When your child reaches the age you were when he or she was born, you’ll stay “twice as old” until your next birthday.

10. Who makes it, has no need of it. Who buys it, has no use for it. Who uses it can neither see nor feel it. What is it? A coffin.
